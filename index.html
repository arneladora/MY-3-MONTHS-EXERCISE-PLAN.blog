Goals

  • Improve strength
  • Enhance cardiovascular fitness
  • Increase mobility and flexibility

Weekly Schedule Overview

Detailed Exercise Plan

Strength Training (3 Days/Week)

  • Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 reps, 60-90 seconds rest
  • Deadlifts: 3 sets of 6-10 reps, 60-90 seconds rest
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 8-12 reps, 60-90 seconds rest
  • Accessory Work: Rows & Curls - 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Cardio & Mobility (2 Days/Week)

  • HIIT: 20-30 min, Example: 30 seconds sprint, 30 seconds walk (repeat)
  • Dynamic Stretching: 10-15 min, full-body movements
  • Foam Rolling: 10 min, major muscle groups

Flexibility & Core (1 Day/Week)

  • Yoga/Pilates: 30-60 min
  • Core Stability Exercises: Planks & Russian Twists

Active Recovery (1 Day/Week)

  • Light Walking: 30-60 min, easy pace
  • Stretching: 15-20 min, major muscle groups

Progression Strategy

  1. Weeks 1-4: Focus on form and gradually increase weights.
  2. Weeks 5-8: Increase intensity by adding weight or reps.
  3. Weeks 9-12: Incorporate variations and increase cardio intensity.

Conclusion

I believe this structured 3-month exercise plan will guide me toward a healthier, more active lifestyle. Combining strength training, cardiovascular workouts, flexibility exercises, and active recovery ensures a balanced approach to fitness.

By following this plan, I can gradually improve my strength, endurance, and flexibility while minimizing the risk of injury. Each phase builds upon the last, allowing for a steady progression that adapts to my body's needs.

Staying consistent and focused on my goals, I am confident that I will make meaningful progress towards a sustainable fitness routine that enhances both my physical health and mental well-being. This journey is not just about physical fitness but also about achieving a balanced and fulfilling lifestyle.
